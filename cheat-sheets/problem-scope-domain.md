# Problem, Scope & Domain

## Problem Statement

**Template:**
```markdown
## The Problem
[Who] needs to [do what] because [why].
Currently, [pain point]. This causes [impact].

## Who has this problem?
- [User type]: [How they experience it]

## Success criteria
- [ ] [Measurable outcome]
```

**Tips:**
- Be specific, not generic
- Talk to real users
- Quantify impact if possible

---

## Scope

**Template:**
```markdown
## In Scope
- [What we WILL build]

## Out of Scope
- [What we WON'T build]

## Constraints
- [Technical/business limits]

## Assumptions
- [What we're assuming]
```

**Tips:**
- "Out of scope" prevents scope creep
- Be explicit about constraints
- Revisit assumptions regularly

---

## Domain Model

**Template:**
```markdown
### [Concept Name]

**Definition**: [What it is in one sentence]

**Properties**:
- [attribute]: [type] - [description]

**Rules**:
- [Business rule]
```

**Tips:**
- Use the language your users use
- Keep definitions short
- Document rules, not just structure
